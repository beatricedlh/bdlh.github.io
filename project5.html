Created for Typography 3, 7-7-7 is a haiku collection that explores disappointment.

Using a selection of Harvard Sentences, I assembled 60 seven-syllable phrases and restructured them into false haikus. The book mimics the format of a traditional poetry collection, featuring images that evoke watercolour—created from archival photos of the Harvard campus in the 1940s.

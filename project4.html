Created for my Graphic Design 4 course, this project was the final reflective piece in a semester-long exploration of the word ommateum. The course asked students to respond to their word through four dimensions.

This final piece, is a circular book made of vellum and cardstock, bound with a Chicago screw. Through layered and distorted imagery, the book explores the shaping of meaning through absence. It reflects on how perception is influenced not just by what is shown, but by what is withheld.

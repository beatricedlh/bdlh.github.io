As part of my Graphic Design 2 course, I developed a website dedicated to learning crochet. The site, fully coded in HTML, CSS, and JS, was designed to offer an engaging experience for Gen Z users interested in crochet, while providing clear and accessible content.

Later, the website content was adapted into a printed zine. Produced using a risograph printer, the paper version featured 8 spreads measuring 4.25” × 5.5” on one side, and an 11” × 17” poster on the reverse.

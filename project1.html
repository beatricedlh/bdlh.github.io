As part of my Typography 2 course, I created a series of posters on Swiss design. The project involved designing three posters for a fictional panel, featuring three major designers: Josef Müller-Brockmann, Max Miedinger, and Mike Joyce.

I used a "paste-up" method to determine the placement of text and colours before recreating the layouts in InDesign.
